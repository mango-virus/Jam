⚔ Arena Battler

Knock everyone off the platform. Last one standing wins.

How to play
  • 🎯 Objective — knock every other player off the platform. Last one standing wins.
  • ❤️ Lives — each player starts with 3 lives. Lose them all and you become a ghost.
  • 👊 Punch — LMB to punch nearby players and send them flying.
  • 🏃 Move — WASD to move, Shift to sprint, Space to jump.
  • ⚔️ Items — weapons and shields randomly appear on the platform. Press E to pick one up. Drop with Z.
    • ⚔️ Sword — stronger knockback, 10 uses
    • 🥊 Boxing Glove — massive knockback, 4 uses
    • 🏏 Home Run Bat — 25% chance for a massive home run hit (with lightning!), otherwise light tap, 1 use
    • 🛡️ Shield — hold RMB to block hits, 7 uses
  • 🛡️ Block — hold RMB to raise your shield and absorb a punch.
  • 👻 Ghost mode — ghosts fly freely and can deliver one massive punch every 7 seconds. Knock an alive player off and you come back with 1 life.
  • 🏆 Armor — the winner earns armor, giving them an extra life in the next round.
  • ⚠️ Tiles — after 25 seconds the platform starts crumbling. Flashing tiles fall away — don't get caught!
