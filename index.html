⚔ Arena Battler

Knock everyone off the platform. Last one standing wins.

How to play
  • 🎯 Objective — knock every other player off the platform. Last one standing wins.
  • ❤️ Lives — each player starts with 3 lives. Lose them all and you become a ghost.
  • 🏃 Move — WASD to move, Shift to sprint, Space to jump.
  • 👊 Punch — LMB to punch nearby players and send them flying.
  • 🛡️ Block — equip a shield then hold RMB to absorb a punch.
  • 👻 Ghost mode — ghosts fly freely and can land one massive punch every 7 s. Knock an alive player off to return with 1 life.
  • 🏆 Armor — the winner earns armor, giving them an extra life next round.
  • ⚠️ Tiles — after 25 s the platform starts crumbling. Flashing tiles are about to fall!
Items & Power-Ups
  • Press E near an item to pick it up  ·  Z to drop
  • ⚔️ Sword — boosts knockback on every hit. 10 uses.
  • 🥊 Boxing Glove — enormous knockback, sends opponents flying. 2 uses.
  • 🏏 Home Run Bat — 25% chance to launch the target skyward with a lightning strike; otherwise a light tap. 1 use.
  • 🚀 Rocket Boots — press Space while airborne for a powerful double jump. Worn on the feet so they don't replace hand weapons. 6 charges.
  • 🛡️ Shield — hold RMB to block incoming hits. 7 uses.
  • Items vanish after 20 s if not picked up. Max 5 on the platform at once.
