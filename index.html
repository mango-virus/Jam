⚔ Arena Battler

Knock everyone off the platform. Last one standing wins.

How to play
  • WASD move  ·  Shift sprint  ·  Space jump  ·  LMB punch / use item  ·  RMB block
  • E pick up item  ·  Z drop item
  • ❤️ You have 3 lives. Lose them all and you become a 👻 ghost — knock a living player off the edge to come back!
  • 🏆 The last player standing wins and receives armor (+1 extra life) for the next round.
  • ⚠️ The platform starts crumbling after 25 seconds — tiles flash before they fall.
Items & Power-Ups
  • E pick up  ·  Z drop  ·  LMB use
  • ⚔️ Sword — Your punches deal extra knockback for 10 hits.
  • 🥊 Glove — 2 punches of massive knockback — enough to send anyone flying off the edge.
  • 🏏 Bat — One swing. 25% chance of a home-run launch that sends the target straight into the void.
  • 🛡️ Shield — Hold RMB to block incoming hits. Absorbs up to 7 hits before breaking.
  • 🚀 Boots — Press Space while airborne for a double jump. 6 charges.
  • 🍌 Banana — Press LMB to drop a peel at your feet. Anyone who steps on it slides uncontrollably!
  • 🕳️ Black Hole Grenade — Press LMB to throw. On landing it creates a 4-second gravity well — players nearby get sucked in, then blasted outward when it detonates.
Random Events
  • 💰 A Loot Goblin Has Appeared! — A speedy goblin dashes around dropping items. Chase it down before it leaps off the edge!
  • ☁️ The Clouds are Alive… — A storm cloud circles the arena firing blasts of wind. Get caught in the gust and you'll be blown off the platform!
  • 🍌 It's Raining Bananas! — Banana peels rain from the sky and coat the platform. Watch every step.
  • 🌋 The Floor is Lava! — Tile drops pause and lava rises from below. Stay airborne — touch the ground and you're eliminated!
  • 🥔 Hot Potato! — A ticking bomb appears above a random player. Hit another player to pass it on. Whoever holds it when it explodes is instantly eliminated — no lives, no respawn!
  • 🍬 Gumball Cannon! — A giant gumball machine crashes down and takes aim at players, firing bubbles that arc to hit you wherever you are. Get hit and you'll be trapped floating helplessly for 2 seconds!
