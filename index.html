⚔ Arena Battler

Knock everyone off the platform. Last one standing wins.

How to play
  • WASD move  ·  Shift sprint  ·  Space jump  ·  LMB punch  ·  RMB block
  • ❤️ 3 lives. Lose them all → become a 👻 ghost. Knock someone off as a ghost to return.
  • 🏆 Winner gets armor (+1 life) next round.  ⚠️ Platform crumbles after 25 s.
Items & Power-Ups
  • E pick up  ·  Z drop
  • ⚔️ Sword — more knockback.  🥊 Glove — huge knockback.  🏏 Bat — 25% chance home run launch.
  • 🛡️ Shield — hold RMB to block.  🚀 Boots — Space mid-air for double jump.  🍌 Banana — LMB to drop a slippery peel.
Random Events
  • 💰 A Loot Goblin Has Appeared! — A fast goblin runs around dropping items. Catch the loot before it jumps off the edge!
  • ☁️ The Clouds are alive… — A friendly cloud circles the arena and blasts gusts of wind. Stay out of the blast or get blown off!
  • 🍌 It's Raining Bananas! — Banana peels rain from the sky and stick to the platform. Watch your step.
  • 🌋 The Floor is Lava! — Tiles stop falling and lava slowly rises from below. Jump to stay alive — touch the ground and you burn!
  • 🥔 Hot Potato! — A bomb appears above a random player's head. Hit someone to pass it before the timer runs out — whoever holds it when it explodes is instantly eliminated!
  • 🍬 Gumball Cannon! — A giant gumball machine drops from the sky and fires huge bubbles across the arena. Get hit by a bubble and you'll be trapped inside for 4 seconds, floating helplessly!
