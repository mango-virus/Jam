⚔ Arena Battler

Knock everyone off the platform. Last one standing wins.

How to play
  • WASD move  ·  Shift sprint  ·  Space jump  ·  LMB punch / use item  ·  RMB block
  • E pick up item  ·  Z drop item
  • ❤️ You have 3 lives. Lose them all and you become a 👻 ghost — knock a living player off the edge to come back!
  • 🏆 The last player standing wins and receives armor (+1 extra life) for the next round.
  • ⚠️ The platform starts crumbling after 25 seconds — tiles flash before they fall.
Items & Power-Ups
  • E pick up  ·  Z drop  ·  LMB use
  • ⚔️ Sword — Extra knockback on punches. 10 hits.
  • 🥊 Glove — Massive knockback. 2 hits.
  • 🏏 Bat — 25% chance of a one-hit home-run launch. 1 swing.
  • 🛡️ Shield — Hold RMB to block. 7 blocks.
  • 🚀 Boots — Space mid-air for a double jump. 6 charges.
  • 🍌 Banana — LMB drops a peel. Enemies who step on it slide off!
  • 🕳️ Gravity Bomb — LMB to throw. Pulls nearby players in for 4s, then explodes outward.
Random Events
  • 💰 Loot Goblin — A goblin drops items as it runs. Catch it before it jumps off!
  • ☁️ Clouds Alive — A storm cloud circles the arena blasting gusts of wind. Don't get blown off!
  • 🍌 Raining Bananas — Peels coat the platform. Watch your step.
  • 🌋 Floor is Lava — Lava rises from below. Stay airborne or get eliminated!
  • 🥔 Hot Potato — A bomb spawns on a random player. Hit others to pass it — whoever holds it when it explodes is out!
  • 🍬 Gumball Cannon — A machine drops and fires aimed bubbles. Get hit and you float helplessly for 2 seconds!
